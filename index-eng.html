My trip from home to University and objects on the way

Conceptual site about what I see and don't see

Website made by: Raimonds Siliņš rs18080.

SLEEP

  Sleep is something that can't be avoided and ignored by even students. The long nights of the session and stress filled mornings are part of our lives. How each one ends up is each one's own responsibility, but I I'm sure I don't do it good as I'd like, because I do most of my day work on the other side of the day and getting into transport to a university is sometimes comparable to the biggest of life achievements. But the journey starts every day just with these feelings I don't really remember already around three. At times a pandemic seems likely to change what is happening in this world, and future generations might not worry about a delaying bus or other transport to an ongoing lecture that could also be listened to in home comfort.
