ROAD

  Part of the next trip is the feelings of driving down the road, which is colored in different textures and potholes. In my case, it's quite different. Including the ground road with the recognizable sound of gravel flight, black and freshly laid asphalt, which completely absorbs the outside sound and creates the pleasurable hum, the bedridden asphalt, who wakes up with tasks to avoid expensive repairs and certainly the path I don't remember, because it didn't matter anyway. The quality of driving is an essential part of the experience and therefore, that the surface of the earth influences my driving speed and consequently the level of stress before university. But of course the best road is that which is not being repaired at the moment, or at least it is marked in “Waze”. Consolidating these factors in one overall answer to where road repair money is and why it is so expensive. It is usually not mentioned but a very important part of even the university's experience. That is why I would also like to show what I see driving along these roads.
